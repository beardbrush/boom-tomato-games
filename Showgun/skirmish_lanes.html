How to score Lanes
How lanes resolve
  • There are 3 lanes. Each side places up to 3 cards per lane.
  • A lane resolves only when both sides have 3 cards in it.
  • Lane total = sum of values + bonus:
  • All same clan: +3
  • Straight (3 consecutive values): +2
  • Higher total claims the lane.
Win condition
  • First to claim 2 lanes wins the match.
