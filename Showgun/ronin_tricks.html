How to score Tricks
How points work
  • Each trick: both play 1 card. Winner scores +1.
  • Mask bonus: each Mask played in the trick adds +1 extra to the trick winner (0–2 bonus).
  • Trump matters, and you must follow the lead clan if you can.
Win condition
  • After 13 tricks, highest total points wins.
