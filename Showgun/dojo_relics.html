How to score Sets
How points work
  • On your turn, take 1 card from the market.
  • Claim a valid 3-card set from your collection:
  • Clan Triplet (all same clan, Masks can help): +5
  • Straight (3 consecutive values, max 1 Mask): +4
  • Pair + Mask (two same-value + exactly one Mask): +3
  • End bonus: leftover Masks score +1 each.
Win condition
  • When deck + market are empty, highest score wins.
