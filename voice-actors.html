Meet the Voice Actors

The voices behind Detective's Notebook

Luke Skelhon

Role: Main Detective

Luke brings our lead detective to life with a mix of weary, grit, and gravitas. Inspired by classic noir, Luke’s delivery sets the tone, bringing you on a journey into each case.

Thomas Young

Role: Street Informer, Mr Soto and Mr Lawson

Tom switches seamlessly between snark and slick, voicing both our underworld informant and our second violinist and Mr Lawson of high standing in the community.

Zoe Bostock

Role: Forensic Expert

Zoe brings a composed, intelligent presence to the forensic expert — her voice steady and assured, even in the face of grim discoveries. With a tone that balances scientific clarity and subtle emotional depth, Zoe guides the player through the psychology of crime scenes and the hidden patterns behind criminal behavior..

Gina B

Role: Ms. Harper (susspect case one) & Penelope Quill (Gossip Journalist)

Gina masterfully switches between icy precision and flamboyant flair. As Ms. Harper, she speaks with composed authority, masking deeper secrets beneath her forensic calm. As Penelope Quill, she unleashes a spirited whirlwind of gossip, wit, and innuendo — never quite letting the truth get in the way of a juicy rumor.
