Meet the Voice Actors

The voices behind Detective's Notebook

Luke Skelhon

Role: Main Detective

Luke brings our lead detective to life with a mix of weary, grit, and gravitas. Inspired by classic noir, Luke’s delivery sets the tone, bringing you on a journey into each case.

Thomas Young

Role: Street Informer, Mr Soto and Mr Lawson

Tom switches seamlessly between snark and slick, voicing both our underworld informant and our second violinist and Mr Lawson of high standing in the community.

Zoe Bostock

Role: Forensic Expert

Zoe's performance is calm and clinical, delivering the professional facts around the criminals and psychological complexity.
